Borgo Lanciano

The workshop will be held at Borgo Lanciano, a beautifully restored historic hamlet nestled in the hills of the Marche region. The venue offers a unique combination of ancient charm and modern conference facilities, providing an inspiring setting for academic exchange.

How to Reach

By Air

The nearest airport is Ancona Falconara Airport (AOI), located approximately 70 km from the venue. From the airport, you can reach Borgo Lanciano by rental car or by taking a train to Fabriano.

By Train

The closest train stations are:

  • Fabriano — approximately 25 km from the venue
  • Ancona Centrale — approximately 70 km from the venue

Fabriano is well connected to major Italian cities via the Rome-Ancona railway line.

By Car

From the A14 motorway (Adriatic coast), take the exit for Ancona Nord and follow directions to Fabriano via SS76. From Fabriano, follow signs to Castelraimondo and then to Borgo Lanciano.
